    • THE PROBLEMS.

    Over the years. Team Fortress 2 has withstood the test of time, with it's last major update of 7 years ago. Players have been wondering when the next major update will be, but that doesn't matter now. Because all they want is to actually play the game without any bots ruining the game for them.

    This page will list the problems that are surrounding Team Fortress 2, starting with:

    1. THE LACK OF UPDATES.

    Worries about the game are now more common within the commuty and what kind of game updates are released, which can cause the game to feel stagnant or unchanging.

    2. THE BALANCE ISSUES.

    There are multiple complaints about the unfairness and enjoyment of the game are harmed by particular classes or weapons being powerful or weak.

    3. THE CHEATING AND HACKING.

    This is by far, the biggest ongoing problem. It involves hacking, cheating, and the effectiveness of anti-cheat systems. This is such a huge problem, that this is what kickstarted the #FixTF2 campaign!

    4. THE COMMUNITY COMMUNICATION.

    There are also a lot of concerns about the lack of transparency in Valve's engagement with the community regarding development plans and difficulties.

    5. THE SERVER PERFORMANCE.

    There are problems with lag, server stability, and other technical problems that affect gameplay.

    6. AND SO MUCH MORE.

    There are many other problems that are affecting the game, but so far. The ones that have been listed here have been prominent within the community.
